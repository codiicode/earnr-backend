How To Earn

Everything you need to know about earning on EARNR

Getting Started

EARNR is a platform where you can earn USDC by completing simple social media tasks. No wallet connect needed - just sign in using your X (Twitter) account.

  • Sign in with your X account
  • Browse available tasks
  • Complete tasks and submit proof
  • Get paid in USDC to your Solana wallet

Task Types

We offer various types of tasks to match your skills and interests:

  • Quote tweets and retweets
  • Likes and follows
  • Content creation (videos, threads)
  • Community engagement
  • And more...

Requirements

Some tasks have minimum requirements to ensure quality:

  • Minimum follower counts (e.g., 25+, 100+, 1000+)
  • Account age requirements
  • Engagement history

Higher requirement tasks typically offer bigger rewards.

Pro tip: Complete tasks quickly! Each task has limited slots, and they fill up fast.

Getting Paid

Once your submission is approved:

  • USDC is sent directly to your Solana wallet
  • No minimum withdrawal amount
  • Fast payouts - usually within 24 hours

Make sure to add your Solana wallet address in your profile settings.

Tips for Success

  • Read task instructions carefully
  • Submit clear proof (screenshots, links)
  • Don't use fake or bot accounts
  • Check back often for new tasks
  • Build your follower count to unlock premium tasks
