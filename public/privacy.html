Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This policy explains how earnr.xyz collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you use earnr.xyz, we collect:

  • X (Twitter) Profile Data: Username, display name, profile picture, and follower count (via X OAuth)
  • Wallet Address: Your Solana wallet address for payments
  • Submissions: Task submissions and proof you provide
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics like page views

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To verify your identity and eligibility for tasks
  • To process task submissions and payments
  • To display leaderboards and activity feeds
  • To prevent fraud and abuse
  • To improve our platform

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data:

  • Publicly: Your username and earnings appear on leaderboards
  • With service providers: Hosting (Vercel), database (Supabase)
  • If required by law

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard practices. We use Supabase for our database with encryption at rest.

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • Access your data through your profile
  • Update your wallet address at any time
  • Request account deletion by contacting us

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising.

7. Third-Party Services

We integrate with:

  • X (Twitter): For authentication
  • Solana: For payments

These services have their own privacy policies.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be announced on our X account.

9. Contact

Questions about privacy? Reach out on X: @EarnrXyz
